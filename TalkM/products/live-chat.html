Live Chat

Live Chat system, a real-time chat messenger widget that helps you to deliver effortless customer support across multiple channels.

Fastest way to engage with your customers

Real-time customer engagements and get to know your customers better, personalize your messages and close sales on the go.

Direct to right advisor or expert

Transfer chat to the right expert to handle customers issue and solve the problems immediately.

Address multiple enquiries instead of phone calls and emails

Customer just click a button and ask whatever they want.

Single representative can chat multiple visitors at the same time

Cost saving by having a single customer support to handle multiple chats.

Responsive and easy access from anywhere in the world

Easy to access from PC or any mobile devices.

Message preview

Allow you to see what your customer types even before he sends the message. Prepare your answers before the customer submits his questions.

Image and attachment sharing

Allow you and your customer to send images and files during the chat.

Customer information

Group your leads and customers based on geography, device and browser type. Target with precision using user properties and events.

Customize your live chat widget and chat window

Choose from a gallery of well-designed images and theme colours to fit your website style and attract your visitors’ attention.

Offline message

When you’re not online, your customer can choose to leave a message and it will be convert to a ticket.

Auto chat assignment

Auto-assign the right conversation to the right team member. You can choose from predefined route options: Predefined Queue Rotation Distribution, Equal Load Distribution and Least Load Distribution.

Chat history

View chat history and all current chats in one screen. Access and filter chat according to specific date and time.

Chat ratings

Learn more from your chats with customer feedback through chat ratings. Easily find chats that need follow-up, and ones that hit it out of the park!

Groups and team management

Manage your internal team members and separate them into respective groups: sales, support, product specialists and more.
